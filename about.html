ADROIT METERING SERVICES LIMITED

From the producer, to the distributor, and to the user, each stakeholder relies on metering and the services around it to play an appropriate role in the overall survival of the business.

All stakeholders in the power sector - the regulator, producers, the transmission company, the distribution companies, IPPs, Mini Grid operators, and ultimately the end consumer – all rely on metering and the services around it to play their roles in the overall survival of the business.

At ADROIT, we understand the metering needs of these stakeholders, and we are well equipped to meet them. We represent a new breed of metering experts in Nigeria today. With over 100 years in combined staff experience, we draw on the best of trainings and resources, and partner with industry leaders to ensure the best of service is delivered to the client!
